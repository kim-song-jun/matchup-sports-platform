- 일반적인 `M`보다 폭이 짧고 밀도가 높아야 한다
- 심볼 하나만 봐도 `브랜드 마크`처럼 느껴져야 한다
- 럭셔리 패션 모노그램처럼 가면 실패, SaaS 기본 로고처럼 가도 실패
- 스포츠 앱인데 지나치게 공격적이면 안 되고, 너무 얌전하면 존재감이 없다

## 추천 생성 세팅

- 출력 포맷: `PNG`
- 사이즈: `1024x1024`
- 배경: `opaque`
- 개수: `1`
- 스타일 키워드: `compact silhouette`, `premium product icon`, `monochrome vector`

## 메인 프롬프트

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: compact premium app icon concept

Brand context:
A trustworthy AI sports matching product for real-world amateur sports communities.
The logo should feel product-grade, calm, clean, and highly scalable.

Primary request:
Create a monochrome app icon concept on a deep navy rounded-square background with one solid white mark.
The mark should subtly suggest an M, but it must feel custom-built, compact, dense, and iconic rather than typographic.
The silhouette should be narrower and more concentrated than a normal letterform.

Background:
Deep navy rounded square.
Premium, calm, clean, not glossy.

Symbol:
Single white shape only.
Rounded corners.
Heavy, stable legs.
A crisp top-center notch that gives the mark character.
The symbol should feel engineered, not decorative.

Shape language:
Dense, compact, stable.
Slightly more serious than playful.
Excellent readability at 16px and 32px.
Minimal but unmistakable.

Brand reference discipline:
Learn only from the silhouette-first discipline and reduction of Apple, Nike, Adidas, Samsung, and Toss.
The output must remain original and must not resemble any existing logo.

Constraints:
No sports equipment.
No orbit.
No shield.
No mascot.
No extra line.
No gradient.
No glow.
No glass.
No bevel.
No texture.
No wordmark.

Output intent:
Centered 1024x1024 PNG app icon concept with one background color and one symbol color only.
```
