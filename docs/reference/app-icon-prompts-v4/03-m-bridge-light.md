## 포지션

- 라이트 배경용 대표 후보
- 웹 favicon, 웹앱 헤더, 앱 아이콘까지 넓게 볼 수 있는 타입
- 모노그램이지만 `연결`의 느낌이 조금 더 살아 있는 방향

## 디자인 의도

- `M`처럼 보여도 되지만, 우선은 하나의 추상 브랜드 심볼이어야 한다
- 상단 구조가 두 기둥을 연결하는 `bridge`처럼 느껴지면 좋다
- 차분하고 오래 갈 수 있는 테크 브랜드 톤을 목표로 한다

## 추천 생성 세팅

- 출력 포맷: `PNG`
- 사이즈: `1024x1024`
- 배경: `opaque`
- 스타일: `light background`, `dark monochrome mark`, `calm premium vector`

## 메인 프롬프트

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: light-background premium app icon concept

Brand context:
An AI-powered sports matching platform that must feel trustworthy, social, and technically refined.

Primary request:
Design a single-color app icon concept on a very light off-white rounded-square background with one dark navy symbol only.
The symbol should feel like a custom monogram-like mark that hints at M and connection at the same time.
The upper structure should imply a bridge or meeting point between two sides.
The final result should read first as an iconic abstract product symbol, not as a normal text character.

Background:
Soft off-white rounded square.
Clean, calm, premium.

Symbol:
Dark navy single-piece mark.
Stable vertical legs.
Distinctive center dip.
Subtle bridge-like top movement.
One unified silhouette with strong negative space.

Shape language:
Slightly wider and calmer than an aggressive sports logo.
Friendly but serious.
Minimal, crisp, and highly scalable.

Brand reference discipline:
Take only the reduction, confidence, and silhouette logic of Apple, Nike, Adidas, Samsung, and Toss.
Do not resemble any existing identity.

Constraints:
No gradient.
No bevel.
No 3D.
No outline.
No extra color.
No mascot.
No ball.
No shield.
No text.

Output intent:
Centered 1024x1024 PNG icon concept suitable for favicon and mobile app icon use.
```

## 네거티브 프롬프트

```text
generic font M, serif monogram, old-school sports logo, crest, ribbon, helmet, ball, corporate clip art, thin line logo, over-detailed geometry, fashion logo, copied nike, copied adidas, copied apple, copied samsung, copied toss
```

## 후속 수정 프롬프트 1

```text
Make the silhouette more memorable and less like a normal letter.
Emphasize the bridge-like upper movement and the unity of the whole mark.
Keep the light background and dark single-color symbol.
```

## 후속 수정 프롬프트 2

```text
Push the symbol toward a timeless product icon.
Keep the mark abstract and calm.
Improve the top connection and the negative space only.
No decorative detail.
```
