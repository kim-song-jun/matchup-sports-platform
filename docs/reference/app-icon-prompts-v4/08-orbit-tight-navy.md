## 포지션

- 오빗 패밀리 중 가장 응축된 버전
- 작은 크기에서 잡히는 밀도가 높음
- `정확도`, `기술`, `컨트롤` 느낌이 가장 강한 타입

## 디자인 의도

- 원형 UI 아이콘처럼 보이지 않도록 링의 밀도와 opening이 중요하다
- 작고 단단하지만 답답해 보이면 안 된다
- `프리미엄 제품 로고`와 `정교한 도구`의 경계에서 전자를 택해야 한다

## 추천 생성 세팅

- 출력 포맷: `PNG`
- 사이즈: `1024x1024`
- 배경: `opaque`
- 스타일: `compact abstract mark`, `tight silhouette`, `navy-white icon`

## 메인 프롬프트

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: compact abstract app icon concept

Brand context:
A premium AI sports matching product that must feel precise, trustworthy, and highly scalable.

Primary request:
Create a compact monochrome app icon concept on a deep navy rounded-square background with one white abstract symbol.
The symbol should be a tighter, denser open orbit with a short inner directional stroke.
The goal is a more compact and iconic silhouette than a normal circular UI icon.
The outer form should feel balanced and engineered.
The inner stroke should feel precise, intentional, and lightly energetic.
This is not a compass and not a radar.
It is a brand mark for a premium consumer product.

Background:
Deep navy rounded square.
Clean and flat.

Symbol:
Compact open ring.
Short inner slash.
Strong density.
Minimal opening.
High small-size readability.

Shape language:
Precise, calm, slightly technical.
Dense but breathable.
Memorable at favicon size.

Brand reference discipline:
Use the reduction and confidence of Apple, Nike, Adidas, Samsung, and Toss only in principle.
The result must remain clearly original.

Constraints:
No sports object.
No map pin.
No instrument dial.
No analytics chart.
No text.
No texture.
No multi-color.
No gradient.
No shadow.

Output intent:
Centered 1024x1024 PNG app icon concept.
```
