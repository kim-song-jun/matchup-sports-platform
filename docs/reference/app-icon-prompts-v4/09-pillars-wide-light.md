## 포지션

- `기둥 + 아치` 계열의 가장 넓고 안정적인 버전
- 문자보다 추상적인 브랜드 심볼을 원할 때 적합
- 팀, 구조, 네트워크, 게이트 같은 연상을 은근히 가져갈 수 있음

## 디자인 의도

- 세 기둥이 보이지만 건축 회사 로고처럼 보이면 실패
- 추상적인 구조감을 주되, 읽을 때는 하나의 통합된 브랜드 마크여야 한다
- 너무 좁으면 문자처럼, 너무 넓으면 건축물처럼 보이므로 균형이 중요하다

## 추천 생성 세팅

- 출력 포맷: `PNG`
- 사이즈: `1024x1024`
- 배경: `opaque`
- 스타일: `abstract structural mark`, `light background`, `dark single-color symbol`

## 메인 프롬프트

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: abstract structural app icon concept

Brand context:
A sports matching platform that connects players, teams, and activities in a trustworthy, structured, mobile-first way.

Primary request:
Design a monochrome app icon concept on an off-white rounded-square background with one dark navy symbol.
Create a custom abstract mark made of three heavy vertical pillars connected by a soft arch-like top form.
The result should feel structured, stable, modular, and memorable.
It may faintly suggest a lowercase m, but it must remain primarily an abstract brand mark.
Think of connection, modularity, meeting structure, or a symbolic gate, not of literal architecture.

Background:
Soft off-white rounded square.
Quiet and premium.

Symbol:
Three heavy vertical forms.
A soft top arch joining them.
Balanced spacing.
One unified silhouette.

Shape language:
Stable, memorable, slightly modular.
Not typographic.
Not architectural.
Strong at small size.

Brand reference discipline:
Use only the reduction, confidence, and modular clarity found in Apple, Nike, Adidas, Samsung, and Toss.
Stay clearly original.

Constraints:
No sports balls.
No shield.
No mascot.
No text.
No gradient.
No tiny detail.
No ornamental cuts.

Output intent:
Flat centered 1024x1024 PNG app icon concept.
```

## 네거티브 프롬프트

```text
building logo, bridge company logo, fintech column icon, government emblem, cathedral arch, architecture studio logo, church symbol, copied adidas stripes, copied apple, copied nike, copied samsung, copied toss
```

## 후속 수정 프롬프트 1

```text
Make the symbol feel more like a brand icon and less like architecture.
Keep the three pillars and the soft top arch.
Strengthen unity and reduce literal structural interpretation.
```

## 후속 수정 프롬프트 2

```text
Push the mark toward an abstract product icon.
Keep the three-pillar logic.
Refine only spacing, arch tension, and silhouette weight.
```
