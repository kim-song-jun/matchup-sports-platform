## 포지션

- 필러 패밀리 중 가장 역동적인 버전
- 스포츠 쪽 에너지를 조금 더 끌어오고 싶을 때 적합
- 다만 `추상 낙서`나 `애슬레저 브랜드`처럼 가면 실패

## 디자인 의도

- 움직임은 있지만 스피드 라인처럼 보여선 안 된다
- 한쪽으로 기울어진 긴장감이 브랜드 개성이 되어야 한다
- 결과는 여전히 앱 아이콘에서 바로 인식되는 큰 덩어리여야 한다

## 추천 생성 세팅

- 출력 포맷: `PNG`
- 사이즈: `1024x1024`
- 배경: `opaque`
- 스타일: `dynamic abstract mark`, `navy background`, `white silhouette`

## 메인 프롬프트

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: dynamic abstract app icon concept

Brand context:
A modern sports matching app that should feel energetic, reliable, and highly productized.

Primary request:
Create a monochrome icon concept on a deep navy rounded-square background with one white abstract symbol only.
Build the symbol from three pillar-like strokes connected by a soft upper sweep.
Introduce a slight forward tilt or directional motion so the mark feels athletic and alive.
The shape may subtly hint at a lowercase m or a modular meeting structure, but it should remain primarily an abstract brand mark.
The silhouette should be bold, unusual, and easy to recognize on a home screen.

Background:
Deep navy rounded square.
Simple and high-contrast.

Symbol:
Three heavy pillar strokes.
A soft connecting top sweep.
Slight forward motion.
One integrated silhouette.

Shape language:
Dynamic but not messy.
Athletic but not aggressive.
Abstract but still product-ready.
Strong in small-size rendering.

Brand reference discipline:
Take only the silhouette discipline and restraint of Apple, Nike, Adidas, Samsung, and Toss.
Do not resemble any specific brand.

Constraints:
No sports objects.
No text.
No gradient.
No shine.
No decorative cuts.
No illustration.
No crest.
No motion blur.

Output intent:
Flat centered 1024x1024 PNG app icon concept.
```

## 네거티브 프롬프트

```text
running figure, wings, streak lines, athletic apparel logo, abstract scribble, architecture logo, mascot, esports mark, jagged motion logo, copied nike, copied apple, copied adidas, copied samsung, copied toss
```

## 후속 수정 프롬프트 1

```text
Keep the dynamic tilt but make the silhouette cleaner and more brand-like.
Reduce any scribble feeling.
Emphasize confidence and clarity over motion.
```

## 후속 수정 프롬프트 2

```text
Make the symbol feel like a global consumer product logo instead of a sportswear mark.
Keep the same dynamic direction.
Refine only the top sweep, pillar spacing, and overall silhouette.
```
