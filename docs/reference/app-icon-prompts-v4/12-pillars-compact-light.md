## 포지션

- 가장 작고 응축된 필러형
- favicon, 작은 앱 배지, mini mark 테스트에 유리
- 과한 존재감보다 오래 가는 정제된 심볼을 원할 때 적합

## 디자인 의도

- 기둥 구조가 보이되 건축 관련 마크처럼 보이면 실패
- 작고 단단하지만 답답하진 않아야 한다
- 작은 크기에서 살아남는 `추상 제품 심볼`이 목표다

## 추천 생성 세팅

- 출력 포맷: `PNG`
- 사이즈: `1024x1024`
- 배경: `opaque`
- 스타일: `compact abstract icon`, `light background`, `dark single-color mark`

## 메인 프롬프트

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: compact abstract app icon concept

Brand context:
A sports matching platform that should feel clean, mobile-first, calm, and trustworthy.

Primary request:
Create a compact monochrome icon concept on a very light rounded-square background with one dark navy abstract symbol.
Build the symbol from three short heavy pillars joined by a compact arch.
The silhouette should be smaller, tighter, and more concentrated than a normal lowercase letter.
It may loosely suggest m, structure, or a meeting gate, but it should read first as a memorable abstract product icon.

Background:
Very light rounded square.
Minimal and premium.

Symbol:
Three short heavy vertical forms.
One compact upper arch.
Dense silhouette.
Strong negative space.
No unnecessary detail.

Shape language:
Calm confidence.
Small-size clarity.
Timeless and restrained.
More product-icon-like than architectural.

Brand reference discipline:
Take inspiration only in principle from the timeless reduction of Apple, Nike, Adidas, Samsung, and Toss.
Do not borrow from any real logo.

Constraints:
No sports gear.
No text.
No gradients.
No extra cuts.
No ornamental styling.
No multiple colors.
Flat and centered only.

Output intent:
Render as 1024x1024 PNG app icon concept.
```

```

```
